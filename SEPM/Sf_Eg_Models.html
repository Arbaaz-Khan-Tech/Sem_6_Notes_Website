2. Agile vs. Traditional (Waterfall) Models

Aspect Agile Waterfall
Flexibility High (adapts to changes) Low (rigid structure)
Customer Involvement Continuous Only at start/end
Testing Ongoing (per sprint) End of project
Best For Dynamic requirements (e.g., startups) Stable requirements (e.g., aerospace)
